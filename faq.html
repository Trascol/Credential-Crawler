General FAQ

Q: What is Credential Crawler?

A: A full-stack resume parser web application, designed to streamline hiring processes and assist students and job seekers by extracting key information from resumes.

Q: How does Credential Crawler work?

A: When a user uploads a resume, the parser extracts key data such as education, work experience, skills, and certifications. It then provides insights and suggestions to help improve the resume.

Q: Who can benefit from Credential Crawler?

A: Job seekers and employers alike can use our system to streamline the hiring process.

For Job Seekers

Q: What file formats can I upload?

A: You can upload resumes in PDF and TXT formats.

Q: How does the keyword extraction work?

A: The system scans resumes for job-relevant keywords based on industry research and employer preferences.

Q: How do I see the results from Credential Crawler?

A: Once the resume is processed, users can view results in their dashboard, showing keyword matches and improvement suggestions.

Q: Can I save different versions of my resume?

A: Yes, you can upload and manage multiple resume versions for different job applications.

For Employers

Q: How many resumes can I upload at once?

A: Employers can upload multiple resumes in bulk using the drag-and-drop feature or file selection.

Q: How do I choose what keywords to search for?

A: Employers can customize their keyword preferences by specifying job titles, required skills, and experience levels.

Q: Can I choose how many resumes it picks?

A: Yes, the system allows employers to set filtering criteria to shortlist a specific number of resumes.
