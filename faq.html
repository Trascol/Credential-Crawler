General FAQ

Q: What is Credential Crawler?

A: A full-stack resume parser web application, designed to streamline hiring processes and assist students and job seekers by extracting key information from resumes.

Q: How does Credential Crawler work?

A: When a user uploads a resume, the parser extracts key data such as education, work experience, skills, and certifications. It then provides key insights and suggestions to help improve your resume. The application also has a login system which helps you keep track of your profile and changes that were made to your resume.

Who can benefit from Credential Crawler?

A: Job seekers and employers alike can use our web development.
